Defconbots 2014 - Tower Defense

Defconbots is back for Defcon 22. After a haitus, we're re-opening under new organizers with a new event at the 2014 convention. The concept of the event is to recreate tower defense games with robots that shoot lasers.

The event

Your goal is to build an autonomous robot with an aiming mechanism and a laser, and be able to shoot the laser at targets moving along a track. Hitting the targets will disable the targets, and when all of the targets are disabled, the wave will end.

Your score will be the number of waves you are able to successfully disable all of the targets. Each wave will get progressively faster, and if necessary, targets will require multiple hits to disable. Final details are forthcoming.

For the full rules, see: https://github.com/Defconbots/2014_Competition_Rules

About us

Jotux and Fryguy originally competed at Defconbots 15 (shooting gallery) before the event ended. We continued to run a similar event at Robogames for the past several years. With Robogames ending, we decided to create a new event and bring it back to the original place: Defcon.
